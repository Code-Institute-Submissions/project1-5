The Monkees

History

The Monkees are an American rock and pop band formed in Los Angeles in 1965. Originally they were four actor-musicians for the American television series "The Monkees" (1966 - 1968). The band was integrated by Americans Micky Dolenz, Michael Nesmith, and Peter Tork; and the British actor and singer Davy Jones.
